The Problem We Are Solving

  • 80% of trade documents are still handled manually
  • Fragmented formats and no standardisation slow down processing
  • Manual workflows introduce delays, errors, and fraud risks
  • SMEs and mid-tier players lack access to digitisation tools used by top-tier banks

That is why we came up with a solution: TradeDocAI.

Who We Are
