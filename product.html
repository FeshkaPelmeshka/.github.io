TradeDocAI

TradeDocAI is an API-native platform that digitises, verifies, and standardises trade documents at scale. It is also accessible via a mobile app.

Core functions include:

  • OCR Image Recognition
  • Key Data Extraction
  • Document Classification
  • Cross-check Engine (multi-document validation)
  • Standards Mapping
  • Structured, verified output

The output is provided in the digital format of your choice, mapped to industry standards (MLETR, ICC DSI, ISO 20022, GS1, WCO DM): JSON, XML, CSV, and more.
